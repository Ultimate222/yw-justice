About YWJ ▼

Youth for Women's Justice (YWJ) was founded by a group of high school students commmitted to bringing awareness and change to the issue of violence against women in Fiji. We believe that education, awareness, and resources are the first steps towards creting a safer and more just society.

Why Focus On Fiji? ▼

In Fiji traditional views on gender roles can make it challenging for women to recognize and address instances of abuse. YWJ aims to break down these barriers by providing cuturally relevant information and resources to empower women and girls to advocate for thier rights.

Core Values

YWJ is built on the core values of Empowerment, Justice, and Community. We believe that every woman has the right ot live free from violence, and we're dedicated to providing the resources and support to make that a reality.

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to educate and support women and girls in Fiji regarding their rights and provide resources to combat gender-based violence.
