About YWJ

YWJ is a platform for amplifying the voices of women and girls, fighting for justice, and fostering empowerment through education and advocacy. Our mission is to create an equitable and inclusive society for all women in Fiji and beyond.

Why Focus on Fiji?

Fiji faces unique challenges, including gender-based violence, economic inequality, and a lack of access to educational resources for women. YWJ is committed to addressing these issues by partnering with local communities, organizations, and leaders to effect change.

Core Values

  • Empowerment: We aim to empower women and girls by educating them about their rights.
  • Awareness: Providing accessible information to promote social change.
  • Advocacy: Advocating for policy changes and societal shifts.
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming all women and girls regardless of background.
  • Community Support: Building a supportive network of allies and advocates.

Purpose

To educate, empower, and advocate for the rights and well-being of women and girls in Fiji.
