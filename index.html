About YWJ ▼

Youth for Women’s Justice (YWJ) is a high school-driven initiative aimed at raising awareness about women’s rights and advocating against violence and discrimination toward women and girls in Fiji. YWJ seeks to inform, empower, and inspire Fijian women and girls to stand up for their rights, recognize signs of abuse, and access the help they need.

Why Focus on Fiji? ▼

Fiji faces unique social and cultural challenges that hinder gender equality. Many women and girls experience violence, harassment, and societal pressures that keep them from recognizing their value and rights. YWJ aims to provide resources tailored to Fiji’s specific cultural contexts and unique needs.

Core Values ▼

Empowerment: Every woman and girl deserves to know her worth and rights. YWJ seeks to empower women by educating them and providing resources.

Awareness: Education and awareness are crucial for social change. YWJ provides accessible information to help individuals recognize key issues and their role in creating a safer society.

Advocacy: Collective action is necessary for women’s rights. YWJ is dedicated to advocating for policy changes and societal shifts to protect women.

Purpose ▼

This site aims to educate and support women and girls in Fiji by providing resources and tools to combat gender-based violence. YWJ strives to foster meaningful change and build a safer, more equitable future.

You Can Help (Get Involved) ▼

Volunteer: Support our campaigns, assist with content creation, or help run events.

Donate: Help fund initiatives that protect and empower women in Fiji.
